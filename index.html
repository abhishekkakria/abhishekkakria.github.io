Background

Why a designer makes a useful marketer

I spent five years making complex things legible — solar farms for municipal approval boards in Germany and the Netherlands, event venues for hospitality buyers, real-estate concepts for investors. Work connected to outcomes like a €200M project-finance facility at Ampyr Solar Europe.

That work is persuasion under constraint: a skeptical audience, one chance, no room for hand-waving. Product marketing is the same discipline applied earlier in the chain — research, segmentation, positioning — and the case studies above are how I practice it. The added advantage: I produce the creative myself, so strategy and execution don't lose anything in translation.

Market research & sizing Competitive intelligence Segmentation & personas Positioning & messaging GTM & launch planning Executive communication Self-produced creative (3D / video / design)
